INTRODUCTION TO BIOMEDICAL ENGINEERING

Biomedical engineering or medical engineering is the application of engineering principles and biology for healthcare purposes(e.g.diagnostic or therapeutic). Biomedical engineering brings engineering and medicine to work together, so to develop machines for healthcare purposes. biomedical engineering is an interdisciplinary course that combines allot of medicine and engineering, students has to be sophisticated in pharmaceutical, anatomy, phsysiology, biochemistry,patological medicine, regenerative medicine, genetic medicine, electrical engineering, computer engineering, mechanic engineering and robotic engineering
